### Strain Theory - All societies have culturally approved goals (American Dream) - Wealth - Social Status - All societies have culturally approved means to achieve these goals: Institutionalized means - Education - Hard work - Problems arise when more emphasis is placed on the goals than the means - "Winning isn’t everything; it’s the only thing!" (UCLA Bruins football coach Henry Russell ("Red") Sanders) - In U.S. culture wealth is more important than intrinsic rewards
### Strain Theory Disadvantaged communities have increased strain - Economic Strain - Fewer job opportunities/lower pay - Family Strain - More single parent households - Educational Strain - Less funding for education - Victimization - Often victims of crime - Discrimination - Impacts all of the other sources of strain [Kaufman 2008](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1375/acri.41.3.421)
