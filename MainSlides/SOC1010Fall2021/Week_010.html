Sex Segregation

  • What is it?
    • The concentration of women in low-paying clerical and service jobs
  • Why does it happen?
    • Socialization impacts what jobs women and men choose to pursue.
    • Men and women want to avoid problems associated with taking jobs traditionally assigned to the other sex.
    • Sex-segregated jobs discriminate against applicants who are the “wrong” sex for that job.

Comparable Worth

  • Important Questions
    • Why do women’s jobs pay less than men’s jobs?
    • Are women’s jobs unimportant?
    • Do women’s jobs require few skills?
  • The Evidence
    • Women’s and men’s jobs may be of equal value and deserve the same pay, but women’s jobs typically pay less than men’s jobs.
    • Women’s jobs pay less than men’s jobs because they are women’s jobs.
    • Even in situations where women and men work in the same jobs, women often earn less than men.
