Early Ideas of Inequality

  1. Karl Marx and conflict theory - Economic Foundation, class division and class conflict
  2. Max Weber - Power, Prestige, and Wealth
    1. Power - The chance of an individual or group to realize their will in social action, even when faced with opposition from others
    2. Prestige - The respect or standing that an individual obtains through appointment or accomplishment
    3. Wealth - Property, Income
