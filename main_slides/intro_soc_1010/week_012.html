Group Activity

Choose 4 and come up with an example for each (at lest 2 from the last 3)
  1. Deindustrialization - social and economic change caused by the removal or reduction of industrial activity
  2. Contingent Worker (precarious work)- provisional group of workers who work for an organization on a non-permanent basis
  3. Glass Ceiling - metaphor used to represent an invisible barrier that keeps a given demographic (typically applied to women) from rising beyond a certain level
  4. Underemployment - an employment situation that is insufficient in some important way for the worker, relative to a standard
  5. Xenophobia - the fear of that which is perceived to be foreign or strange
  6. Power Elite Model - By C. Write Mills - people who occupy the dominate positions in institutions (i.e. military, economic, political) their decision have enormous consequences
  7. Pluralist Model - power in a society is derived from the representation of diverse groups
  8. Emotional Labor - requirement of a job that employees display required emotions toward customers or others
