Group Activity

pick 4 and come up with an example for the terms

  1. Aversive Racism - Negative evaluations of racial/ethnic minorities are realized by a persistent avoidance of interaction with other racial and ethnic groups.
  2. Implicit Bias - Jennifer Eberhardt - Relatively unconscious and relatively automatic features of prejudiced judgment and social behavior.
  3. Laissez-Faire Racism - Blames minorities for their poorer economic situations, viewing it as the result of cultural inferiority
  4. Color-Blind Racism - Eduardo Bonilla-Silva - The disregard of racial characteristics when selecting which individuals will participate in some activity or receive some service
  5. Institutional Racism - Form of racism expressed in the practice of social and political institutions
  6. White Privilege - Societal privileges that benefit white people in Western countries beyond what is commonly experienced by non-white people under the same social, political, or economic environment
