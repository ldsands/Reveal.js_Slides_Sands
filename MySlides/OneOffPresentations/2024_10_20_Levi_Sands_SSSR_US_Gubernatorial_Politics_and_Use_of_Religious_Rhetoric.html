## US Gubernatorial Politics and Use of Religious Rhetoric Levi Sands, MA, University of Iowa (levi-sands@uiowa.edu)
##
## Data - Project Vote Smart's (PVS) database of political speeches - Included all gubernatorial candidates for all elections from 2004 through March of 2023 - Data is from 10 states selected to represent a diversity of states in size, political control and regions of the country (AL, AZ, CA, ID, IL, ND, NY, RI, TX, WA) - I used the Press Release, Speech, and Statement categories for a total of 34,192 "speeches" - For the purposes of analysis I split the data into sentences using pysbd (855,440 total sentences)
## Religious/Christian Dictionary - I used religious terms (mostly christian) then using regular expressions filtered
