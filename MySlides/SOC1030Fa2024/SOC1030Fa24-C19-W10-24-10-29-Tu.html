## Elements of Gender Relations - Seven important elements of transformations of gender relations - Legal Rights - Labor force participation - Occupational Structure & Income - Power - Family structure - Domestic division of labor - Sexuality
## Legal Rights Gained by Women - Own/manage property when husband incapacitated (PA-1718, ME-1821) more over the next few decades - Illinois and New York First to ban abortion after quickening (1827) - Right to vote 19th Amendment (1920) - Equal right to divorce (gradually since 1940s) - Maybe eroding in the future - Equal rights to university admission (1960s) - Equal rights to all jobs (1960s) - Equal rights to participate in sports (1972) - Reproductive rights (1973, but eroding) - SB8 (Texas) (Sep 1, 2021) - Dobbs v. Jackson Women's Health Organization (Dec 2021, May & June 2022) - Equal Credit Opportunity Act (1974) - More or less the last of equal rights with regard to property and money (other than pay discrimination) - Lilly Ledbetter Fair Pay Act (2009) - Ledbetter v. Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co. (2007) - Equal pay for equal work - Equal right to participate in all military roles (2016)
